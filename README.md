## 📂 Project Directory (Table of Contents)

### 1. [Telecommunications Customer Churn (Classification)](1_Telco_Customer_Churn)
* **Industry:** Telecommunications
* **Description:** Engineered a predictive classification model to identify high-risk customers likely to cancel their subscriptions. Conducted rigorous EDA and feature engineering to isolate the primary drivers of churn, allowing business units to proactively deploy retention strategies.

### 2. [Aviation Satisfaction Analysis (EDA & Predictive Modeling)](2_Aviation_Satisfaction_Analysis)
* **Industry:** Aviation / Transportation
* **Description:** Analyzed massive airline passenger datasets to determine the key factors driving customer satisfaction and dissatisfaction. Built data-driven business intelligence reports to help airlines optimize flight services and improve passenger retention.

### 3. [Agriculture Crop Recommendation Engine (Machine Learning)](3_Crop_recommendation)
* **Industry:** Agriculture / Agritech
* **Description:** Developed a predictive machine learning algorithm that analyzes soil metrics (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) and environmental conditions (temperature, humidity, rainfall) to recommend the optimal crop for maximizing agricultural yield.

### 4. [Streaming Sentiment Analysis (NLP)](4_Streaming_Sentiment_Analysis)
* **Industry:** Media / Streaming
* **Description:** Developed a Natural Language Processing pipeline to ingest unstructured text data (movie/show reviews). Engineered text-cleaning functions (regex, stopword removal) and converted human text into mathematical matrices to accurately predict positive vs. negative user sentiment.

### 5. [Healthcare Insurance Cost Prediction (Regression)](5_Insurance_Cost_Regression)
* **Industry:** Healthcare / Insurance
* **Description:** Engineered a multivariate regression algorithm to predict continuous, non-linear medical insurance costs based on overlapping patient demographics and compounding risk factors (like BMI and smoking status). 

### 6. [E-commerce Image Classification (Deep Learning / CV)](6_fashion-mnist)
* **Industry:** Retail / E-commerce
* **Description:** Built a Computer Vision Neural Network to process raw pixel matrices. The model automatically categorizes unstructured product images into distinct retail departments, streamlining automated e-commerce inventory tagging.

### 7. [Marketing Customer Segmentation (Unsupervised Learning)](7_Marketing_Customer_Segmentation)
* **Industry:** Marketing / Advertising / Finance
* **Description:** Deployed the Elbow Method and a K-Means clustering algorithm to analyze a massive banking database. Mathematically discovered and mapped 4 distinct, high-value target audiences (Marketing Personas) to optimize mass-marketing campaign ROI.
